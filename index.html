About Krishi Sakha

Krishi Sakha is an innovative platform developed for the International Agricultural Hackathon, designed to bridge the information gap between farmers and markets.

Our mission is to empower farmers with real-time data on crop prices, weather conditions, and market trends to help them make informed decisions and maximize their profits.
